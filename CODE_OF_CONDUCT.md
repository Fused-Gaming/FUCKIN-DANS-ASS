# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community focused on blockchain security research and criminal investigation.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- **Respectful Communication** - Being kind and courteous in all interactions
- **Constructive Feedback** - Providing helpful, actionable suggestions
- **Collaboration** - Working together toward common security goals
- **Professional Conduct** - Maintaining professionalism in all contexts
- **Learning Mindset** - Being open to learning from others
- **Evidence-Based** - Focusing on facts and verifiable data
- **Privacy Respect** - Protecting sensitive investigation details
- **Legal Compliance** - Ensuring all work follows legal guidelines

### Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- **Harassment** - Including offensive comments, personal attacks, or trolling
- **Doxxing** - Publishing private information without consent
- **False Attribution** - Falsely labeling addresses or creating fake evidence
- **Illegal Activity** - Using tools for unauthorized surveillance or illegal purposes
- **Bad Faith** - Deliberately disrupting investigations or spreading misinformation
- **Retaliation** - Retaliating against those who report violations
- **Spam** - Flooding with irrelevant or promotional content

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, including:

- GitHub repository (issues, PRs, discussions)
- Project documentation
- Community channels
- Public representation of the project

It also applies when an individual is representing the project in public spaces.

## Reporting

### How to Report

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it:

1. **Email:** `conduct@fused-gaming.com`
2. **Include:**
   - Description of the incident
   - Date and location
   - Names of individuals involved
   - Witnesses (if any)
   - Any supporting evidence

### Confidentiality

All reports will be handled with discretion. We will protect the identity of reporters unless required by law.

### Response Timeline

- **Acknowledgment:** Within 24 hours
- **Investigation:** Within 7 days
- **Resolution:** Within 14 days

## Enforcement

### Investigation Process

1. **Receipt** - Maintainer acknowledges report
2. **Review** - Investigate the incident thoroughly
3. **Determination** - Assess severity and context
4. **Action** - Implement appropriate consequences
5. **Communication** - Notify involved parties

### Consequences

Violations may result in:

1. **Warning** - Official warning with explanation
2. **Temporary Ban** - Temporary suspension from community
3. **Permanent Ban** - Permanent removal from community
4. **Legal Action** - In cases of illegal activity

The severity of consequences depends on:
- Nature of the violation
- Pattern of behavior
- Impact on community
- Response to correction

## Special Considerations for Forensic Work

### Ethical Investigation

When conducting blockchain investigations:

- **Verify Evidence** - Ensure all attributions are factually accurate
- **Protect Privacy** - Don't dox individuals unnecessarily
- **Legal Bounds** - Stay within legal investigative limits
- **Responsible Disclosure** - Follow proper disclosure procedures
- **Source Citations** - Always cite evidence sources

### Attribution Standards

When tagging addresses:

- **Accuracy** - Only tag with verifiable information
- **Context** - Provide adequate context and evidence
- **Updates** - Correct attributions if new evidence emerges
- **Severity** - Use risk levels appropriately

### Data Handling

- **Confidentiality** - Respect sensitive investigation details
- **Sharing** - Only share findings through appropriate channels
- **Storage** - Securely store investigation data
- **Deletion** - Properly dispose of sensitive data when done
